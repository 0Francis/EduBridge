About EduBridge

EduBridge is a dedicated platform for youths and organizations. Youths can showcase their skills, search for jobs posted by NGOs and companies, and apply to roles that match their qualifications. Organizations can post job openings to find talented individuals ready to make an impact.

Feature 1: Post Your Skills

Youths can create profiles to highlight their skills and experiences, making it easy for organizations to discover them.

Feature 2: Browse and Apply for Jobs

Explore job listings from NGOs and organizations, filter by your skills, and apply directly through the platform.

Feature 3: Job Posting for Organizations

Organizations and NGOs can post available jobs, review applicant skills, and connect with qualified youths seamlessly.
